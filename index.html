Why Choose Thika Talents

An affiliate of St. Alicia & Talents Group of Schools, we are committed to transforming positively every learner through their talents and interests at a very individualized level.

Holistic Growth

We nurture learners holistically, making them self-reliant, efficient, and effective to their families, community, and the entire world.

Professional Excellence

Our core values of professionalism, integrity, and excellence guide every aspect of our educational approach.

Value Creation

We foster an environment of love, courtesy, and non-discrimination, creating value for our students and community.

Prime Location

Located in the heart of Thika town at Imara Plaza, 4th Floor, opposite Kanini Haraka near the Main Stage.

TVET Accredited

As a registered TVET institution, we provide recognized qualifications that prepare students for successful careers.

Individual Focus

We transform positively every learner in our institution irrespective of their backgrounds through their talents or interests.
